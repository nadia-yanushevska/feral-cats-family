Adoption Information

All cats in our care have been fixed and microchipped, dewormed, and treated for mites and fleas or any major diseases.

Anyone interested in adoption must fill out an application and meet the cat first. After the meet-and-greet, you should take some time to decide whether you think this cat would be a match for you - there is no rush.

Unlike most rescues, we offer our support and guidance even after the adoption to make sure your new cat settles in well and develops a long-lasting relationship with you as well as your family and friends.
